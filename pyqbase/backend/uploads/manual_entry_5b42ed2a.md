# UPSC CAPF 2023

Q1. Which of the following is considered an indirect tax in India?
A. Corporation Tax
B. Income Tax
C. Goods and Services Tax (GST)
D. Wealth Tax

Answer: C

Q2. The 'Great Bath' was discovered at which of the following Indus Valley Civilization sites?
(a) Harappa
(b) Mohenjo-Daro
(c) Lothal
(d) Dholavira

Answer: B
